about us

why we bottle slow

We steep every batch by hand, in small runs, over three to five days — never rushed, never scaled past what one person can watch closely. Our oils are cold-pressed, our clays are sun-dried, and nothing leaves the studio until it's been tested on our own skin first.

We believe good skincare takes patience, not shortcuts. From slow-infused botanicals to hand-poured bottling, we lean on old techniques because they still work best — whether it's a single-note facial oil or a three-step evening ritual.

From your first morning cleanse to the last step before bed, we want every bottle to feel like it was made for exactly you. Come find the ritual that fits your skin.
